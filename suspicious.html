Suspicious sites

This page shows the suspicious activities pertaining to your information leakage. Note that it might be not correct. You can set the threshold of suspicious and more dangerous actions on the settings pages.
"Suspicous" means that the login attempt occurs more than a certain threshold. It may not be serious but still requires to be watched carefully.
"Warning" indicates possibly more dangerous situation, which means the use of your personal information exceeds the your pre-determined threshold. Please check whether there are some malicious codes on your computer if "Warning" shows up here.
Default threshold for "suspicous" and "warning" is 10 and 20, respectively.
The status is decided by the data that occur today.
